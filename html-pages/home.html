About Us

Farmers aren't technology experts. WE ARE.

A unique team of researchers, developers, and entrepreneurs with versatile backgrounds, making our combined value unique to the edible insect industry.

Bug Mars was founded in early 2021, during a global pandemic that highlighted environmental and supply chain issues. Our vision is to empower and transform our food landscape. We use technology to improve supply reliability, meeting growing demand for sustainable protein sources and reducing our ecological impact one insect at a time.
